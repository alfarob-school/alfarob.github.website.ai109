its # Introduction

- Social work helps people handle difficult life situations
- Social workers support individuals, families, and communities
- I chose this career because it combines compassion, advocacy, and public impact

# Career Overview

- Social workers help with poverty, trauma, mental health, housing, and family instability
- They work in schools, hospitals, government agencies, and community organizations
- Common tasks include case management, referrals, counseling, and documentation

# Important Skills

- Communication and active listening
- Empathy and interpersonal trust
- Problem-solving and organization
- Ethical judgment and professionalism

# How AI May Disrupt Social Work

- AI can speed up paperwork and routine administrative tasks
- It may be used for scheduling, summaries, screening, and case review
- Agencies may rely more on digital systems and data tools

# Risks of AI in This Field

- Biased data can lead to unfair decisions
- Privacy and confidentiality may be at risk
- Automated systems should not replace professional judgment

# Benefits of AI

- Saves time on notes, reports, and repetitive tasks
- Helps organize information and compare resources faster
- Can support better planning and clearer communication

# What AI Cannot Replace

- Building trust with clients
- Responding to crisis with compassion
- Understanding emotional and family dynamics
- Making ethical decisions in complex situations

# Gaining an Advantage

- Learn to use AI as a study and organization tool
- Use AI to improve writing, presentations, and research summaries
- Build AI literacy with a strong ethical mindset

# My Action Plan

- Explore different social work specialties
- Practice using AI responsibly for schoolwork and research
- Create a portfolio with strong written and presentation pieces
- Get real experience through volunteering or community work

# Conclusion

- Social work is a deeply human career
- AI will likely support the field, not replace it
- Human empathy, ethics, and advocacy will remain essential
